Help Us by Donating!

Thank you in advance for your continued support of our agency with your generous donations. Your donations help us maintain the highest level of training and best equipment possible so that we are ready when you need us the most!

Our commitment to the community is to provide the BEST level of emergency medical care and this commitment requires people, training, equipment and, needless to say, money. Although a portion of our operating funds come from billed services for transport, your donations are still critical to our organization. Ettrick Matoaca Volunteer Rescue Squad is a 501(c)(3) organization. Each and every dollar is sincerely appreciated — we can’t thank you enough.
