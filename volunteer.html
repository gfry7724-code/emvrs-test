About Volunteering

Some members come to us already trained in the EMS field as firefighters and EMTs, while others join with no previous experience and receive training. Our volunteers come from a wide range of backgrounds including high school students, college students, nurses, school bus drivers and technology consultants.

Ettrick-Matoaca Volunteer Rescue Squad needs your help! We will provide you with all the necessary training—at our cost—to help you learn how to save lives.
