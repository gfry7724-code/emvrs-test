Ettrick-Matoaca Volunteer Rescue Squad

Proudly serving South Chesterfield since 1976, the Ettrick-Matoaca Volunteer Rescue Squad is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization providing emergency medical services to the community 24/7. Our team of trained volunteer EMTs responds to 911 calls with dedication, compassion, and professionalism.

Basic Life Support (BLS)

Our BLS providers are the front line of emergency care. They assess and treat a wide range of medical emergencies, including CPR, bleeding control, splinting fractures, and administering key medications for breathing difficulties, chest pain, or diabetic events.



Advanced Life Support (ALS)

ALS providers deliver more intensive care such as cardiac monitoring, IV access, and advanced medication administration. EVOC-certified members safely operate our ambulances and assist in high-level patient care during transport.
